About Us

Crebit is a centralized farm related loan rendering service.The main concept of our website is blockchain.It is a progressing, transparent and profitable technology.We're linking with the bank to promote a secure and trustworthy interface that credits the farmers digital money.Together we strive to promote an imperative change that can affect million of lives in coming years.

How it Works

Register shopkeepers and service providers related to farming with us

Register customers and provide them with farming loans

Our digital currency can be used only with the registered shops and service providers

When a user is registered,a chain responsible for their transactions is initiated

For every transaction made,a block is added to user's chain

Farmers can repay their loans using cash and with remaining digital currency

Shopkeepers can exchange their digital currency with cash

A chain is completed only when all the digital currency finally returns to the bank.
